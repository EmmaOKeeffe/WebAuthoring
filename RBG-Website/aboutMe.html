The final assignment for my Web Authoring module was to make a website about my Hero. For my her I picked U.S. Supreme Court justice, Ruth Bader Ginsberg. The reason I picked Ruth as my hero is that I think she was a really inspiring and empowered woman who paved the way for many women behind her in terms of equal rights. Although I don't have any interest in law, I watched a documentary about Ruth shortly after her death and hadn't realised how such a petite and seeminly quiet woman could be so outspoken and passionate about fighting for everyone to have equal rights. Her active support of the LGBTQ+ community attempting to give a voice to the people who felt voiceless did not go unseen and was greatly appreciated by all in minority groups. She not only just spoke of helping but also donated her 1 million dollar award winnings across 60 charities. Her hard work and determined attitude is something eveyone could be inspired by and her prescence in the world will be greatly missed.
